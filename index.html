Our League

GAB LICENSE

Approved and licensed under Games and Amusements Board.

1ST IN PH

The very first Professional Chess League in the Philippines and in the whole world.

TEAMS

There are 24 teams in the PCAP as of the inaugural season divided into two geographic groups: the Northern and Southern Divisions.

MATCHES

Each match includes 7 boards divided into 4 categories — top-rated, lady, senior, and homegrown.

PLAYERS

Teams can have 8 to 10 players under contract and must be licensed by the Games and Amusements Board (GAB).

TOURNAMENTS

A total of 3 conferences each season, starting with the All-Filipino Conference in January.
